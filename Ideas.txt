-- Player Mechanics --
Different attack types
  - Melee
    + Daggers: Fast, Short Range, Low Damage, High Movement
    + Sword: Jack of all Trades, Master of None
    + Hammer/Greatword: Slow, High damage, Large Area of Attack
  - Projectile
    + Bows:
      - Short Bow: Fast, Accurate, Multiple Projectiles, Movement
      - Long Bow: Long-Medium Range, Slow, Pierce, High Damage
    + Magic:
      - Elemental: Fire, Water, Earth, Wind, etc
      - Casting Slows Player
      - Relies on Mana, Mana is From Kills

Player Abilities:
  - Absorb slimes for their power?

-- Enemy Mechanics --
Slime Types
  - Green: Basic slime. Easy to kill. Not much loot.
  - Red: Tougher than green, drops a little more loot.
  - Blue: Resistant to magic (Shoots projectiles?)
  - Brown: Tanky slime. High health and higher-than-average damage, but slow.
  - Yellow: Fast bois
  - Purple: Boss type. Stats are much higher than other slimes, but they spawn in fewer numbers.

Slime Mechanics:
  - When slimes collide, they combine into a more powerful entity.
  - Slimes of the same type have their healths added, then that new value is used for the new entity.
  - Slimes of different types have half of their stats combined.
